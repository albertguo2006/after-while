"Cosmic reckoning."

This image represents Lucky’s pain and hurt. On the left side, the heart-shaped nebula is split in two by a red streak, indicating the hurt that Arnya’s actions have had on Lucky. The colours have deep meaning as well. Red is the colour of blood, and often has a complex meaning. It can represent love, death, pain, anger, and many other emotions and concepts. This is representative of the complex relationship between Lucky and her mother. It is seen in the story that Arnya is cold towards her daughter, but there is still some hint of love and care. Although she does not show her emotions well, it can be assumed that she experiences a complex set of emotions due to her fragmented relationship.
