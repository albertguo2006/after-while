"Lucky felt a pinch in her stomach and the familiar feeling of guilt."

In the context of this story, the stop sign in a forest symbolizes internal doubts and questioning of Lucky. Arnya's unpredictable lifestyle leaves her daughter with a sense of instability and uncertainty. The stop sign symbolizes the moments where Lucky hesitates or doubts herself, unable to fully embrace her potential and capabilities because of the emotional roadblocks her mother has inadvertently placed in her path. Just as the stop sign disrupts the natural flow of the forest, these pressures disrupt Lucky's emotional growth and ability to navigate her world with confidence.
